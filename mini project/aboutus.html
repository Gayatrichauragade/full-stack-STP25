Exploring the Natural Wonders of India, One Yatra at a Time

Who We Are

Yatra in India is a travel platform dedicated to showcasing the breathtaking natural beauty of India. From the snow-capped peaks of the Himalayas to the serene backwaters of Kerala, our mission is to take you closer to India’s most stunning landscapes and peaceful getaways.

Our Mission

We believe that nature heals and inspires. Our mission is to help people explore India’s incredible natural destinations — responsibly, respectfully, and with a sense of wonder.

What We Value

  • Nature First: Promote eco-tourism and preserve the beauty of India’s forests, rivers, mountains, and coastlines.
  • Authentic Experiences: Encourage meaningful travel to untouched and less crowded places.
  • Local Support: Empower local communities through responsible tourism.
  • Sustainability: Encourage minimal waste and eco-friendly practices during travel.
  • Peaceful Escapes: Help people reconnect with nature and themselves.

Why Travel With Us?

Our handpicked destinations include hidden waterfalls in Meghalaya, peaceful valleys in Himachal, sunset beaches in Goa, and tea gardens in Assam. Whether you seek calm, adventure, or inspiration — we’ve got a nature-filled yatra waiting for you.
